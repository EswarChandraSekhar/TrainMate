Who We Are

TrainMate is India's first dedicated lost and found reporting platform exclusively built for Indian Railway passengers.

Whether you're someone who misplaced a valuable item or a kind-hearted passenger who found something, TrainMate provides a safe and admin-verified platform to report and reconnect items with their rightful owners.

We believe in building trust, reducing conflict, and promoting honesty throughout our railway journeys.

“A single act of honesty can reunite a journey with its peace.”
